hello world

My name is Justin Owen and I am a math graduate student at Texas A&M. My main research focus is on solving eigenvalue problems for operators defined on surfaces via the finite element method. I enjoy the interplay between differential geometry, PDEs, and numerical methods. Recently, I've also become interested in Bayesian inverse problems and machine learning (like everyone else).

When I'm not working on research, teaching, or reading math, I can be found at the gym lifting weights, watching movies, or grabbing a beer with friends. I also enjoy hiking where I sometimes stumble across real world tent functions like in the photo above.
