Machine Learning

This project was focused on a priori error estimates for surface finite element approximation of eigenfunctions and eigenvalues of the Laplace-Beltrami operator. This scenario diffes from the Euclidean case because approximation of the geometry must be accounted for. During this project we stumbled upon a novel way to exploit the approximation of the geometry to control superconvergence of the eigenvalues.
